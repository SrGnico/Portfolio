01 Price Control App

(the app is not yet in the play store, I'm working on it but you can still download the app by pressing the first button above.)

Add, delete, sum and charge!

This application is designed for small businesses that want to keep their prices up to date and controlled in a pocket-sized app.

In Price Control you can add products, edit them, delete them, when charging you can scan, add manual price, increase quantities and choose the payment method.

Then you can control your sales in the day, with each category, total and more information in an orderly manner.

Main fetaures:

- Add, edit or delete products.

- Scan or add price manually.

- Collect transfer or cash

- Keep detailed daily log.

- Modify quantity in cart.

- Filter records by date.

- Filter products by category or search.

- Change the price of several products at once.
